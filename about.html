My Mission

Jeffrey Zhang

Before beginning my studies in Computer Science at Cornell University, I went through a series of personal explorations to uncover my "perfect" major. I came to the conclusion that what I choose would not define my future, rather it will be my grit, self-determination, and personal ambition that will dictate my success and place in society. My passion lies in the intersection of Entrepreneurship and Computer Science with a strong focus on discovering technological gaps in the market and executing on opportunities. I strive to continuously personal improvement with a vision to become a leader in the technological revolution, inspiring and uplifting others along my journey.
