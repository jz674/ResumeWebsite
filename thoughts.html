My Realizations

I am fortunate!

I really mean it when I say I am fortunate. My family came to America empty-handed. We were at rock bottom, so the only direction to go was up. From a young age, I witness my mother single handedly taking care of me while working a 9-5 job. My father was the purest definition of a workaholic, leaving the house at 5am, coming home at 2am, and sleeping in during the weekends; I rarely saw him. After graduating high school, I discovered new found appreciation and admiration for my parents' sacrifices. They are my biggest role models. And even though they're not perfect, I love them!
