My Realizations

I am fortunate!

I really mean it when I say I am fortunate. My family immigrated to America with $30,000 debt - mom and dad worked night and day tirelessly for years to start from 0. We were at rock bottom, so the only direction to go was up.

From my earlier memories, my mother single-handedly took care of me and my little sister while working 9-5 waiting tables. My dad would leave the house before dawn and come back at 2am - pure workaholic. I rarely saw him, yet I understood he had to do what was best for the family.

After graduating high school, I discovered new found appreciation and admiration for my parents' sacrifices. They are my biggest role models. And even though they're not perfect, I love them!
