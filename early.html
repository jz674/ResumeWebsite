Fam(ily)

My family is reason I am the man today, showing me that you don't need a lot to do a lot, I was taught that the accurate metric of my self worth is not how I feel about my positive experiences, rather it's how I respond to my negative ones. Of course, I still have my insecurities — we all do — but now my focus is on building my professional portfolio and forming positive relationships with my love ones.
